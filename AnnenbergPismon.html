Descendants of Dad and Leah Pisom

 

The late Walter Annenberg was the founder of TV Guide as well as other magazines. He was also United States Ambassador to England from 1969 to approx 1974.

Moses Annenberg, Walter's father, was a mobster during the 1920's and 1930's. He took over the Daily Racing Form.

Some of the Name's came from a Biography of Moses and Walter Annenberg. Some of the information come from various websites. The rest of the information came from Family members and well as the 1900, 1910,1920 and 1930 United States Census as well as Ellis Island.
