Revolutionary Voices: A Last Muster Film   

I am on a mailing list for Maureen Taylor,  Photo Detective.  The below is part of an email I received on October 5,2012

 For the last ten years I've worked on The Last Muster project, trying to locate images of individuals alive during the American Revolution but who lived into the age of photography. It's been a real challenge.  Volume 2 in the series will be available in 2013.

The really exciting news is that I've partnered with Verissima Productions, an award-winning documentary film company to bring the story of the Last Muster to life.

We have a website LastMusterFilm.com.  I could use your help spreading the word. Watch the website for updates on our progress.
