Stein Photo Page

 

The below is a photo of the Sister of Annie Stein Stone. Annie is  from Pluisk, probably in the Warsaw area of Poland. Annie and her family came to the UK circa 1892 and settled in the Mile End area of London where she married  Isaac Woolf in 1894. 

On some of Annie's children's birth certificates, her maiden name was given as  Hanna Stein, Annie  Stone, Chana Camyen. ( Camyen could be the wrong spelling, but perhaps spelt as it sounded. I believe that Kamen means stone in Polish !!)

Her sister, the one in the picture, is from Hereford in the United Kingdom. The picture was taken in the Bronx, NY.  It looks like the picture was taken during the 1920's and is possibly a wedding picture.

This was part of an email received by me on December 25,2007:

My grandmother Annie didn't go to America, but funnily enough a sister of hers did, and I believe married there. I have a photo of her in what looks like bridal wear taken at the Goldstein Studios, Bronx. Unfortunately I don't have her name, but if you want me to send the photo to see if she looks like any of your Stone/Stein Family, you are welcome.
