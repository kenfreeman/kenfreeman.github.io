Information about Annenberg Marble

 

Moses (my great grandfather) was also the owner of M Annenberg Marble Works in Brooklyn, NY.

Sol (my grandfather and a son of Moses)  was the owner of Annenberg Marble in Jackson Heights, NY.

 

This company was owned by my grandfather, Sol Annenberg until his death in 1981. His father, Moses Annenberg, had owned a predecessor company, M Annenberg Marble Work ( I think that was the name of the company).
