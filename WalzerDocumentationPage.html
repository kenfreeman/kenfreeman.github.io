David Annenberg and Carole Freeman Family History and Genealogy Website

 

This is some of the Documentation we have for the Walzer and Waltzer  Families.

 

My research also show's they were Furriers. Sam, Max and Herman were the furriers.  Sam's wife Anna and there son's Charles and Maxwell owned 'A Walzer and Son' located in New York, NY.  Herman owned a company, 'H Walzer & Co' in Chicago, ILL.
