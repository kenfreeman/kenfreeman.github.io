Descendants of Mottis Novoselsky and Ethel (Novoselsky)   


           

The purpose of this page is to show the link between the Novoselsky  family and the Kirshner and Jacobson families and how they relate to Carole and David Annenberg.  It is interesting to note that in addition to their marriage, they have two common relatives.  

Perry London is Sylvia Shyken's first cousin.  Sylvia's husband is Jacob Kirshner who's sister, Thelma, is Web mistress's Carole's mother. 

Perry's wife, Vivian Jacobson is the 3rd cousin to Web Master David's father, Alfred Annenberg.
