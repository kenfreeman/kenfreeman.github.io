This is the 1st time David appears in the Newspaper.

It was on February 15,1966 in the Journal American Brooklyn Section.

 

David's father, Alfred Annenberg, was sworn in as an Assistant District Attorney in Kew Gardens, Queens County New York City New York. I was trying to take my own picture and the photographer must have seen something he liked and the enclosed picture. It turns out that my camera was not working properly and this picture is the only one I have of the event.
