 Descendants of Dad Vigransky and Tillie (Virgransky)

 

 

According to Joseph Freeman's 1936 Social Security Application, mom Emma's maiden name is Harris.

According to Emma's 1915 Death Certificate, her father's name is N.R. (Not Readable) Vigransky and  her mom's name is Tillie Unknown. The name of Vigransky came from her Death Certificate.

The family seems to have come from the Poland Russia area and then settled in New York City.  Elkano was married twice according to Census records and Emma is  his 2nd wife.
