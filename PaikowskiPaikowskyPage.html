Descendants of Mordekhai and Nekhama Pikovski

 

A lot of the  information came from the book 'There Once was a World A 900 Year Chronicle of the Shtetl of Eishyshok'  by Yaffa Eliach as well as Yad Vashem and family interviews.

The family came from the Shetel Eishyshok, Poland. At that time, Eishyshok was a Shetel near Vilner Poland. It is know near Vilnuis Lithuniana.
