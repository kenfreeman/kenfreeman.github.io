David Annenberg and Carole Freeman Family History and Genealogy Website
 

 

Descendants of Harris Friedman and Sarah Frankel
 

 

The only information we have is that the family is from Russia. Census Records show that Elkano Friedman was married twice. Nothing is known about his first wife.

Original name according to the 1900 Census is Friedman.  Joseph changed his name to Freeman. The 3 youngest, including Joseph,  were born in New York and the rest in Russia.
