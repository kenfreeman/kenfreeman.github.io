Descendants of Anthony Flaherty and Mary Lydon

 

Family came from Aran Islands, County Galway, Ireland and went to Boston, Massachusetts and married into the Manning Family.

We do not activley track this family as the Margaret Flaherty that married Patrick Joseph Mannng was his first wife and not our direct line.

Patrick Joseph Manning's second wife, Mary Flaherty is our greast grandmother and she is the one we are tracking.
