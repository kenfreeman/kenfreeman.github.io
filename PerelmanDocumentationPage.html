This is the Documention page for the Perelman Family.

 

Sindey Joseph (SJ) Perelman was, according to Wikipedia, an American humorist, author, and screenwriter. In cinema, Perelman is noted for co-writing scripts for the Marx Brothers films Monkey Business (1931) and Horse Feathers (1932), and for the Academy Award-winning screenplay Around the World in 80 Days (1956).
