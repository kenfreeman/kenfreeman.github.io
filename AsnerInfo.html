Ansner Family Information Page

This page is the family of the Actor Ed Asner. His Grandfather's sister Rachel married Israel Berkowitz, one of my maternal Great Grandmother's brother's.

   The information on this page came from a variety of sources including but not limited to: the Paikowsky family, various website's that mention Ed Asner, the United States 1920 and 1930 Census, Marquis Who's Who, a book "There Once was a World" A 900 year Chronicle of the Shtetl of Eishyshok"  by Yaffa Eliach  and Michael Jon Neill's website http://www.rootdig.com/ which provided the information about the 1930 US Census.
