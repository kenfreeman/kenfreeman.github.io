This is our Shyken Information Page

 

We do not actively track this family

The Shyken Family is related to both the Kirshner and Jacobson family.   A Shyken, Perry London, married a Jacobson, Vivian Jacobson. Perry's 1st cousin, Sylvia Shyken is married to Carole's Uncle, Jack Kirshner and Vivian is David's father's 3rd Cousin.
