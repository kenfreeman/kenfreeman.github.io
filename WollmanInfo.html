This is the Wollman Information Page                 

 

The Wollman Family, is headed by Elias Adolph Wollman and his wife Annie Stone Stein.

Our research says that Elias came from either Eckoonen Russia or Memmel Germany. Annie seems to have come from either Germany or Russia.

The Annenberg's, Boneparth's and Cohen's all married into the Wollman Family. 2 Wollman sister's married 2 Cohen Brother's.
