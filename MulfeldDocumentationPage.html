Mulfeld Documentation Page

 

The family came from Vienna and Lemberg, Austria and Russia according to various Census records and various Ellis Island records as well as marriage certificate of Emil Mulfeld and Sonya Rovinsky / Roven

 We have researched the Mulfeld family and documented the through the following methods;  Family interviews, a Marriage Certificate,  US Census records found at the US National Archive's, the Godfrey Library, Ancestry.com, Footnote.com,  Ellis Island Records found at www.EllisIsland.org and Social Security Information found Ancestry.com

 

Additional Information can be provided upon request.
