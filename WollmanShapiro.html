Descendants of Shepsel Wollman and Mom Shapiro

 

Some of this information came from the 1920 United States Census.

Elias Wollman came to the United States and moved to Brooklyn, NY. All his children were born in Brooklyn. Elias was a toy and fireworks maker.

The name on Elias's Death Certificate was: Elias Adolph Wollman.  On the 1920 Census it was: Adolph Elias Wollman.

One of his grandson's  was a lawyer, Assistant District Attorney and a Department Head, for Queens County, NY and an Assistant Attorney General for the State of New York.  Another was a Car Dealer. Another grandson  was Mayor of Glen Cove, Long Island NY and a Nassau County, Long Island, NY Family Court Judge.

In addition,  one of his great grandson's was the producer of the Television show 'The Baseball Bunch'.
