New York City Public Libraries

.Brooklyn Daily Eagle at the Brooklyn Public Library

New York City Public Library  They have in the Library the Microfilmed Index's of what is available at the NYC Municipal Archives.   They also have on Microfilm, the 1790-1930 Census for New York State and certain other states. They also have on Microfilm various city directories.  In the room nearby, the have various  Index's in Book form for certain States for the Federal Census from 1790-1910. They do not seem have an 1880 Index in Book form. Please visit the website or give them a call for more detailed information.
