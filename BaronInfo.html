                                                                    Baron Information Page

 

The family had it's start in Stanislaw Poland.  Member's of the family also came from Tyrolean Austria. My Grandmother, Dorothy Jacobs nee Schussler,  had moved to Hanover Germany while she was a child.

The family had moved around before the war and most of them were lost in WW2.

Current family members are living  in Israel,  Ontario Canada and the United States.
