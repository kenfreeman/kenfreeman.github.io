This is our Shlansky Shlanski Information Page

 

 This page is about the  Shlanksy / Shlanski family that came from Vilner and Eishyshok, Poland and Eishyshok and Vilnius Lithuania.

 

Some of the information  concerning the Shlansky family came from the book 'There Once was a World A 900 Year Chronicle of the Shtetl of Eishyshok'  by Yaffa Eliach and  'Tradition and Modernism in the Shtetl Aisheshuk 1919-1939'  by Ellen Livingston.
