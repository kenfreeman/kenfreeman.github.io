David Annenberg and Carole Freeman Family History and Genealogy Website
 

 

                             Descendants of Avraham Mordekhai  Shlanski and Zisle Gordon.

 

 

The Shlansky's and Shlanski's  are from Vilner, Poland and Eishyshok, Poland. and we think they are related either  to the Berkowitz family or the Yanklevich  from Vilner and Eishyshok, Poland. The links are at the bottom of the page.

A lot of the  information came from the books 'There Once was a World A 900 Year Chronicle of the Shtetl of Eishyshok'  by Yaffa Eliach. and 'Tradition and Modernism in the Shtetl Aisheshuk 1919-1939'  by Ellen Livingston.

Other information came from family members as well as the online version of the New York Times Database. That information includes Obituaries, Marriage Announcements and Birth Announcements.

I am not sure of the accuracy of the below information, especially the dates. I am only sure of Morris and his son Elliot as I have met them.
