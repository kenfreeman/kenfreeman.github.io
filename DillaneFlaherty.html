Descendants of Mary Dillane and Michael John Flaherty

 

The Flaherty Family came from the Aran Islands, County Calway Ireland and settled in Liverpool England before moving to the Boston Massachusetts area.

The Dillane Family came from Liverpool England and also went to the Boston Massachusetts area.
