Jacobs Yanklevich Documentation Page

 

 We have researched the Jacobs and Yanklevich family and documented the through the following methods;  Family interviews, a Marriage Certificate,  US Census records found at the US National Archive's, the Godfrey Library,  Ancestry.com,  Ellis Island Records found at www.EllisIsland.org , Naturalization Records found at www.footnote.com and Social Security Information found at Mocavo

 

Information can be provided upon request.
