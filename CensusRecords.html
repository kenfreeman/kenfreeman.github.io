This is our Census Records page.

 

Please keep in mind that my file is set up with the Maiden name of the woman or the married name in parenthesis ()  if the  maiden name is not known. The women's names on the  below files, in Adobe format, can either be the Maiden or Married Name. 

We will be posting Selected Census Records on the pages listed below.  Some of the other Census Records that we have are posted on the below listed Family Documentation Page's

Please note that is most case's, most of the time we use the Head of Household when you look at the Census  Records. The exceptions, which are rare, are for our parents or grandparents  or a specific family member that we are looking for.
